Chicken Katsu Sandwiches

Matt Anderson

2022-03-07 20:34

Source

I still have a bunch of coleslaw from the Impromptu BBQ Potluck so my roommate suggested we make spicy chicken katsu sandwiches. The original recipe uses lettuce and a spicy pickle mayo, but I think the coleslaw was even better.

We bought extra thighs for an upcoming meal which I deboned and deskinned. I'll save the bones for chicken stock and the skin for schmaltz.
