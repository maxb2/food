Halal Cart-Style Chicken and Tzatziki

Matt Anderson

2022-03-09 12:17

Source

My roommate has a favorite tzatziki recipe he likes to make. When he told me he was going to make that and nothing else, I decided to help him round out the meal. I made the chicken and rice from Serious Eats' Halal Cart-Style Chicken and Rice with White Sauce and a Tabouli salad. I made the rice with some homemade chicken stock as well. May favorite part of the meal is the chicken. The marinade is really excellent!
